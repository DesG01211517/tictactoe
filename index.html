DIRECTIONS:

The objective of Tic Tac Toe is to get three of your symbols (traditionally "X" or "O") in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, on the 3x3 grid. Any space labeled "OPEN" is Available for either player. GOOD LUCK!!!
